The Artist

A studio built on patience, precision and care

Blush & Bloom began the way most good things do — with people asking Vishakha to do their nails and mehndi at home instead of the salon down the road. What started as appointments for friends has grown into a private studio where every client gets the same unhurried attention.

Every design is planned around your hands, your skin, your occasion — not a fixed catalogue. Bridal mehndi is booked weeks in advance and hygiene is non-negotiable: sanitised tools, single-use files, and fresh products for every client.
